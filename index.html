We're a social group for women who make things. From bloggers, to seamstresses, to designers, to developers, to welders and DIYers. We welcome any women who consider themselves makers, as well as the people who support them.

Our first meetup will be February 4, 2016 at Strongbox West, and you can RSVP through MeetUp. Check back later to learn more or tweet us for more information.

We currently only meet in Atlanta, GA, but we're interested in starting new chapters once we grow.

Membership in the Society of Lady Makers is open to anyone who identifies as female and considers themselves to be a maker, as well as anyone who supports women in DIY, STEM, design, technology, and the arts.

Society of Lady Makers is dedicated to providing a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of gender, gender identity and expression, sexual orientation, disability, physical appearance, body size, age, race, or religion. We do not tolerate harassment of participants in any form.

This code of conduct applies to all Society of Lady Makers spaces, both online and off. Anyone who violates this code of conduct may be sanctioned or expelled from these spaces at the discretion of the Society of Lady Makers founders.

Some Society of Lady Makers spaces may have additional rules in place, which will be made clearly available to participants. Participants are responsible for knowing and abiding by these rules.

This code of conduct was made from an open source template provided by http://geekfeminism.org and inspired by The Ada Initiative.
